# <span class="header-definition">Definition</span>

## Dihedral Group $D_n$^[이면체군]

정 $n$각형 (regular $n$-gon)의 모든 **symmetries^[대칭]**의 group

$$D_n = \text{symmetries of regular } n\text{-gon}$$

**Order**: $|D_n| = 2n$

**원소**:
- $n$ rotations^[회전]
- $n$ reflections^[반사]

## Geometric Description^[기하학적 설명]

정 $n$각형을 자기 자신으로 mapping하는 rigid motions^[강체 운동]:

### Rotations^[회전]

$$r, r^2, r^3, \ldots, r^{n-1}, r^n = e$$

$r$: Rotation by $\displaystyle\frac{2\pi}{n}$ counterclockwise^[반시계방향]

**각도**: $0°, \displaystyle\frac{360°}{n}, \displaystyle\frac{2 \cdot 360°}{n}, \ldots, \displaystyle\frac{(n-1) \cdot 360°}{n}$

### Reflections^[반사]

$$s, sr, sr^2, \ldots, sr^{n-1}$$

$s$: Reflection through an axis

**Axis**: 
- $n$ odd: Through vertex and midpoint of opposite edge
- $n$ even: Through two opposite vertices OR midpoints of opposite edges

## Presentation^[표시]

$$D_n = \langle r, s \mid r^n = s^2 = e, srs = r^{-1} \rangle$$

**Generators**: $r$ (rotation), $s$ (reflection)

**Relations**:
1. $r^n = e$ (order of rotation)
2. $s^2 = e$ (order of reflection)
3. $srs = r^{-1}$ (conjugation relation)

**Alternative**: $sr = r^{-1}s$

## Abstract Definition^[추상적 정의]

Group generated by two elements $r, s$:
- $r$ of order $n$
- $s$ of order 2
- $srs = r^{-1}$

**모든 원소**:
$$D_n = \{e, r, r^2, \ldots, r^{n-1}, s, sr, sr^2, \ldots, sr^{n-1}\}$$

## Normal Form^[정규형]

Every element uniquely written as:

$$r^i s^j \quad \text{where } 0 \leq i < n, \; j \in \{0, 1\}$$

**두 가지 경우**:
- $j = 0$: $r^i$ (rotations)
- $j = 1$: $r^i s$ (reflections)

# <span class="header-examples">Examples</span>

## Example 1: $D_3$ (Equilateral Triangle)

정삼각형의 대칭군

**Order**: $|D_3| = 6$

**원소**:
- **Rotations**: $e, r, r^2$ ($0°, 120°, 240°$)
- **Reflections**: $s, sr, sr^2$ (3 axes)

**Isomorphism**: $D_3 \cong S_3$ ✓

정삼각형의 꼭짓점을 permute하는 것과 같음!

### Cayley Table

| $\circ$ | $e$ | $r$ | $r^2$ | $s$ | $sr$ | $sr^2$ |
|---------|-----|-----|-------|-----|------|--------|
| $e$ | $e$ | $r$ | $r^2$ | $s$ | $sr$ | $sr^2$ |
| $r$ | $r$ | $r^2$ | $e$ | $sr^2$ | $s$ | $sr$ |
| $r^2$ | $r^2$ | $e$ | $r$ | $sr$ | $sr^2$ | $s$ |
| $s$ | $s$ | $sr$ | $sr^2$ | $e$ | $r$ | $r^2$ |
| $sr$ | $sr$ | $sr^2$ | $s$ | $r^2$ | $e$ | $r$ |
| $sr^2$ | $sr^2$ | $s$ | $sr$ | $r$ | $r^2$ | $e$ |

## Example 2: $D_4$ (Square)

정사각형의 대칭군

**Order**: $|D_4| = 8$

**원소**:
- **Rotations**: $e, r, r^2, r^3$ ($0°, 90°, 180°, 270°$)
- **Reflections**: $s, sr, sr^2, sr^3$ (4 axes)
  - 2 through opposite vertices
  - 2 through midpoints of opposite edges

**Subgroups**:
- $\langle r \rangle = \{e, r, r^2, r^3\} \cong C_4$
- $\langle r^2 \rangle = \{e, r^2\}$
- $\langle s \rangle = \{e, s\}$
- $\langle sr \rangle = \{e, sr\}$
- $V_4 = \{e, r^2, s, sr^2\}$ (Klein four-group)

## Example 3: $D_5$ (Regular Pentagon)

정오각형의 대칭군

**Order**: $|D_5| = 10$

**원소**:
- **Rotations**: $e, r, r^2, r^3, r^4$ ($0°, 72°, 144°, 216°, 288°$)
- **Reflections**: $s, sr, sr^2, sr^3, sr^4$ (5 axes through vertices)

**Properties**:
- $\langle r \rangle \cong C_5$ (cyclic of order 5)
- No subgroup of order 4 (Lagrange)

## Example 4: $D_6$ (Regular Hexagon)

정육각형의 대칭군

**Order**: $|D_6| = 12$

**원소**:
- **Rotations**: $e, r, \ldots, r^5$ ($0°, 60°, 120°, 180°, 240°, 300°$)
- **Reflections**: 6 axes
  - 3 through opposite vertices
  - 3 through midpoints of opposite edges

**Rich subgroup structure**: Many interesting subgroups

## Example 5: $D_2$ (Rectangle, Special Case)

정사각형이 아닌 직사각형... Wait, $D_2$는 정2각형?

Actually, $D_2$는 조금 특별함:

$$D_2 = \{e, r, s, rs\}$$

where $r^2 = s^2 = e$

**Isomorphism**: $D_2 \cong V_4 \cong C_2 \times C_2$ (Klein four-group)

**Abelian!** (유일한 abelian dihedral group $\neq D_1$)

## Example 6: $D_n$ as Subgroup of $S_n$

정 $n$각형의 꼭짓점에 번호 $1, 2, \ldots, n$:

$$D_n \leq S_n$$

**예**: $D_4 \leq S_4$
- $r = (1234)$
- $s = (24)$
- Generates 8-element subgroup of $S_4$

## Example 7: Reflection Axes

### $n$ Odd

모든 reflection axes가 한 vertex와 opposite edge의 midpoint를 통과

**예**: $D_3$, $D_5$, $D_7$

### $n$ Even

두 종류의 axes:
- $n/2$ axes through opposite vertices
- $n/2$ axes through midpoints of opposite edges

**예**: $D_4$, $D_6$, $D_8$

## Example 8: Non-Example (Rotation Group)

**Rotation group만**: $C_n = \langle r \rangle \leq D_n$

Reflections 제외, rotations만

**Order**: $n$ (not $2n$)

**Always cyclic and abelian**

## Example 9: Infinite Dihedral Group $D_\infty$

"Infinite strip"의 대칭:

$$D_\infty = \langle r, s \mid s^2 = e, srs = r^{-1} \rangle$$

**No relation** $r^n = e$!

**Order**: $|D_\infty| = \infty$

**Structure**: Fundamental group of Klein bottle (related)

## Example 10: Wallpaper Groups

일부 wallpaper groups는 dihedral groups 포함:

**p2mg, p2mm, p3m1, p4m, p6m** 등

Crystallographic symmetries

# <span class="header-properties">Properties</span>

## Non-Abelian (Except $D_1, D_2$)

**정리**: $D_n$은 $n \geq 3$일 때 non-abelian

**증명**: $rs = sr^{-1} \neq sr$ (for $n \geq 3$)

**특수한 경우**:
- $D_1 = C_2$ (trivial case, abelian)
- $D_2 \cong V_4$ (Klein four-group, abelian)
- $D_3 \cong S_3$ (non-abelian)

## Rotations Form Normal Subgroup

$$\langle r \rangle = \{e, r, r^2, \ldots, r^{n-1}\} \triangleleft D_n$$

**Index**: $[D_n : \langle r \rangle] = 2$ (normal because index 2!)

**Quotient**:
$$D_n / \langle r \rangle \cong C_2$$

**Proof of normality**:
- $r^i r r^{-i} = r$ (rotation conjugates to rotation)
- $s r s^{-1} = srs = r^{-1} \in \langle r \rangle$

## Center^[중심]

$$Z(D_n) = \begin{cases} \{e\} & \text{if } n \text{ is odd} \\ \{e, r^{n/2}\} \cong C_2 & \text{if } n \text{ is even} \end{cases}$$

**$n$ even**: $r^{n/2}$ = 180° rotation (commutes with all)

**$n$ odd**: No non-trivial center

## Subgroups^[부분군]

### Cyclic Subgroups

- $\langle r \rangle \cong C_n$ (rotations, index 2)
- $\langle r^d \rangle \cong C_{n/d}$ for $d \mid n$
- $\langle s \rangle \cong C_2$ (single reflection)
- $\langle sr^i \rangle \cong C_2$ (other reflections)

### Dihedral Subgroups

$d \mid n$에 대해:
$$\langle r^d, s \rangle \cong D_{n/d}$$

**예**: $D_6$에서
- $\langle r^2, s \rangle \cong D_3$
- $\langle r^3, s \rangle \cong D_2$

## Conjugacy Classes^[켤레류]

### $n$ Odd

1. $\{e\}$
2. $\{r, r^{-1}\}, \{r^2, r^{-2}\}, \ldots$ (rotation pairs)
3. $\{s, sr, sr^2, \ldots, sr^{n-1}\}$ (all reflections together)

**Total**: $\displaystyle\frac{n+3}{2}$ conjugacy classes

### $n$ Even

1. $\{e\}$
2. $\{r^{n/2}\}$ (180° rotation)
3. $\{r^i, r^{-i}\}$ for $0 < i < n/2$ (rotation pairs)
4. $\{s, sr^2, sr^4, \ldots\}$ (vertex reflections)
5. $\{sr, sr^3, sr^5, \ldots\}$ (edge reflections)

**Total**: $\displaystyle\frac{n+6}{2}$ conjugacy classes

## Automorphism Group^[자기동형군]

$$|\text{Aut}(D_n)| = \begin{cases} n\phi(n) & \text{if } n \text{ is odd} \\ 2n\phi(n) & \text{if } n \text{ is even} \end{cases}$$

where $\phi$ is Euler's totient function

**복잡한 구조**: Not just inner automorphisms

# <span class="header-theorem">Theorem</span>

## Classification of Order $2n$ Groups

**Not all** groups of order $2n$ are dihedral!

**Counter-example**: Quaternion group $Q_8$ (order 8)
- $|Q_8| = 8 = 2 \cdot 4$
- But $Q_8 \not\cong D_4$

**Difference**:
- $D_4$: Multiple elements of order 2
- $Q_8$: Only one element of order 2 ($-1$)

## Dihedral Groups in Galois Theory

$D_n$은 특정 polynomials의 Galois groups

**예**: $x^n - 1$ (over suitable fields)의 Galois group과 관련

자세한 내용은 [[Galois Theory]] 참조

## Semidirect Product Structure

$$D_n \cong C_n \rtimes C_2$$

Semidirect product of $C_n$ and $C_2$

**Action**: $C_2$ acts on $C_n$ by inversion

$$s \cdot r \cdot s^{-1} = r^{-1}$$

## Presentation Uniqueness

Presentation $\langle r, s \mid r^n = s^2 = e, srs = r^{-1} \rangle$은 $D_n$을 **완전히 결정**

Different $n$ $\Rightarrow$ non-isomorphic groups (except $D_3 \cong S_3$)

# <span class="header-remark">Remark</span>

## 직관적 이해

**Dihedral Group**: "정다각형의 대칭"

**메타포**:
- **거울**: Reflection symmetry
- **회전**: Rotational symmetry
- **Snowflake**: Natural dihedral symmetry
- **Logo design**: Many logos have dihedral symmetry

**핵심**: Rotation + Reflection

## Why "Dihedral"?

**이름의 유래**: Greek "di" (two) + "hedra" (faces)

"Two-sided" = 평면 도형 (vs polyhedra)

**역사적**: 평면 정다각형의 symmetries

## Notation Warning^[표기 주의]

일부 책에서 $D_{2n}$을 사용 (order로 표기):

| 본 노트 | 일부 책 | Order |
|---------|---------|-------|
| $D_3$ | $D_6$ | 6 |
| $D_4$ | $D_8$ | 8 |
| $D_n$ | $D_{2n}$ | $2n$ |

**본 노트**: $D_n$ = 정$n$각형의 대칭 (vertices 개수로 표기)

## Dihedral vs Cyclic

| | **$C_n$** | **$D_n$** |
|---|---|---|
| Order | $n$ | $2n$ |
| Generators | 1 (rotation) | 2 (rotation + reflection) |
| Abelian | Yes | No ($n \geq 3$) |
| 기하학 | Rotations only | Rotations + Reflections |

**관계**: $C_n = \langle r \rangle \leq D_n$ (subgroup of index 2)

## 표기법

| 표기 | 의미 |
|------|------|
| $D_n$ | Dihedral group ($n$ vertices) |
| $r$ | Rotation generator |
| $s$ | Reflection generator |
| $\langle r \rangle$ | Rotation subgroup |
| $C_n \rtimes C_2$ | Semidirect product structure |

## Common Pitfall^[흔한 실수]

### 1. Order of $D_n$

✗ $|D_n| = n$?

✓ $|D_n| = 2n$ (rotations AND reflections)

### 2. $D_n$ is abelian?

✗ Only $D_1 \cong C_2$ and $D_2 \cong V_4$

✓ $D_n$ is non-abelian for $n \geq 3$

### 3. All order $2n$ groups are dihedral?

✗ Counterexample: $Q_8$ (order 8)

✓ Many other groups of order $2n$ exist

### 4. Reflection composition

✗ $s \cdot s = s^2 = ?$

✓ $s^2 = e$ (반사 두 번 = 항등원)

✗ Two different reflections?

Depends! May be rotation or another reflection

### 5. $D_3 = S_3$?

✓ Yes! $D_3 \cong S_3$ (isomorphic)

But: $D_n \not\cong S_n$ for $n \geq 4$
