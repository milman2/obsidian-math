# <span class="header-definition">Definition</span>

## Abelian Group^[아벨 군]

Group $G$가 **abelian**^[아벨] 또는 **commutative**^[가환]이다 $\Leftrightarrow$

$$\forall a, b \in G, \quad ab = ba$$

**동의어**: Commutative group^[가환군]

### 표기법

Abelian group의 연산은 주로:
- **Additive notation**: $a + b$, identity $0$, inverse $-a$
- **Multiplicative notation**: $ab$, identity $e$, inverse $a^{-1}$

**관례**: 명시적으로 abelian인 경우 additive notation 사용

### Non-Abelian Group^[비가환군]

$$\exists a, b \in G \text{ such that } ab \neq ba$$

**예**: Most groups are non-abelian!

# <span class="header-examples">Examples</span>

## Example 1: Integers $\mathbb{Z}$

$$(\mathbb{Z}, +)$$

**연산**: 덧셈

**확인**: $a + b = b + a$ for all $a, b \in \mathbb{Z}$ ✓

**성질**:
- Infinite cyclic group
- Generated by $1$

자세한 내용은 [[Cyclic Group]] 참조

## Example 2: Integers Modulo $n$

$$\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z} = \{[0], [1], \ldots, [n-1]\}$$

**연산**: $[a] + [b] = [a+b]$

**확인**: Additive notation (abelian)

**위수**: $|\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}| = n$

## Example 3: Real Numbers $\mathbb{R}$

$$(\mathbb{R}, +) \quad \text{and} \quad (\mathbb{R}^\times, \cdot)$$

where $\mathbb{R}^\times = \mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}$

**Both are abelian**

## Example 4: Vector Spaces

모든 **vector space**^[벡터 공간]는 additive group으로 abelian

$$V = \mathbb{R}^n, \quad v + w = w + v$$

**일반화**: Module over a ring

자세한 내용은 [[Linear mapping]] 참조

## Example 5: Direct Product of Abelian Groups

$$G_1, G_2 \text{ abelian} \Rightarrow G_1 \times G_2 \text{ abelian}$$

**연산**: $(g_1, g_2)(h_1, h_2) = (g_1h_1, g_2h_2)$

**확인**: Component-wise commutativity ✓

## Example 6: Klein Four-Group

$$V_4 = \{e, a, b, c\} \cong \mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z}$$

**Cayley table**:

| | e | a | b | c |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **e** | e | a | b | c |
| **a** | a | e | c | b |
| **b** | b | c | e | a |
| **c** | c | b | a | e |

**Symmetric**: Abelian ✓

## Example 7: Quotient of Abelian Group

$G$ abelian, $H \leq G \Rightarrow G/H$ abelian

**증명**: $(g_1H)(g_2H) = (g_1g_2)H = (g_2g_1)H = (g_2H)(g_1H)$ ✓

## Example 8: Non-examples

### Symmetric Group $S_n$ ($n \geq 3$)

$$(12)(13) = (132) \neq (123) = (13)(12)$$

**Not abelian**

자세한 내용은 [[Symmetric Group]] 참조

### Dihedral Group $D_n$ ($n \geq 3$)

$$rs \neq sr \quad (r \text{ rotation}, s \text{ reflection})$$

**Not abelian**

자세한 내용은 [[Dihedral Group]] 참조

### General Linear Group $\text{GL}_n(\mathbb{R})$ ($n \geq 2$)

$$AB \neq BA \text{ (일반적으로)}$$

**Not abelian**

# <span class="header-properties">Properties</span>

## All Subgroups are Normal

**정리**: $G$ abelian $\Rightarrow$ 모든 subgroup이 normal

**증명**: $H \leq G$, $g \in G$, $h \in H$

$$ghg^{-1} = hgg^{-1} = h \in H$$

따라서 $gHg^{-1} = H$ ✓

**결과**: Abelian group에서는 모든 subgroup으로 quotient 가능

자세한 내용은 [[Normal Subgroup]] 참조

## Center equals the Whole Group

**정리**: $G$ abelian $\Rightarrow Z(G) = G$

$$Z(G) = \{z \in G : zg = gz \; \forall g \in G\}$$

**증명**: $G$ abelian이므로 모든 원소가 중심에 속함 ✓

**역**: $Z(G) = G \Rightarrow G$ abelian ✓

## Commutator Subgroup is Trivial

**Commutator subgroup**^[교환자 부분군]:

$$G' = [G,G] = \langle [a,b] = aba^{-1}b^{-1} : a,b \in G \rangle$$

**정리**: $G$ abelian $\Leftrightarrow G' = \{e\}$

**증명**: 
- ($\Rightarrow$): $ab = ba \Rightarrow aba^{-1}b^{-1} = e$ ✓
- ($\Leftarrow$): $G' = \{e\} \Rightarrow$ all commutators are identity $\Rightarrow$ abelian ✓

## Quotient by Commutator Subgroup

**Abelianization**^[아벨화]:

$$G^{\text{ab}} = G/G'$$

**정리**: $G^{\text{ab}}$는 항상 abelian

**의미**: $G$의 "가장 큰 abelian quotient"

**Universal property**: 모든 $G \to A$ (to abelian) factor through $G^{\text{ab}}$

## Fundamental Theorem of Finitely Generated Abelian Groups

Finitely generated abelian group $G$는:

$$G \cong \mathbb{Z}^r \times \mathbb{Z}/n_1\mathbb{Z} \times \cdots \times \mathbb{Z}/n_k\mathbb{Z}$$

where $n_i \mid n_{i+1}$ (invariant factors)

또는 (Primary decomposition):

$$G \cong \mathbb{Z}^r \times \mathbb{Z}/p_1^{a_1}\mathbb{Z} \times \cdots \times \mathbb{Z}/p_m^{a_m}\mathbb{Z}$$

**Parameters**:
- $r$: rank^[계수] (free part)
- $n_i$ or $p_i^{a_i}$: torsion part^[비틀림 부분]

자세한 내용은 [[Isomorphism]] 참조

## Torsion and Free Part

**Torsion subgroup**^[비틀림 부분군]:

$$\text{Tor}(G) = \{g \in G : \exists n \in \mathbb{N}, \; g^n = e\}$$

**Free part**^[자유 부분]: $G/\text{Tor}(G) \cong \mathbb{Z}^r$

**정리**: Finitely generated abelian group

$$G \cong \text{Tor}(G) \times \mathbb{Z}^r$$

자세한 내용은 [[Group]] 참조

## Hom and Tensor

Abelian groups form a **category**^[범주]:

- **Morphisms**: Group homomorphisms
- **Products**: Direct sum $\oplus$
- **Hom**: $\text{Hom}(A, B)$ is abelian group
- **Tensor**: $A \otimes B$ exists

**Category**: $\mathbf{Ab}$ (category of abelian groups)

자세한 내용은 [[Category Theory]] 참조

## Schur's Lemma (for abelian groups)

$A, B$ simple abelian groups

$$\text{Hom}(A, B) = \begin{cases}
\mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z} & \text{if } A \cong B \cong \mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z} \\
0 & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}$$

**Simple abelian groups**: $\mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z}$ only (prime order)

# <span class="header-theorem">Theorem</span>

## Classification of Finite Abelian Groups

**정리**: Finite abelian group $G$는 cyclic groups의 direct product:

$$G \cong \mathbb{Z}/n_1\mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}/n_2\mathbb{Z} \times \cdots \times \mathbb{Z}/n_k\mathbb{Z}$$

### Invariant Factor Form^[불변 인자 형태]

$$n_1 \mid n_2 \mid \cdots \mid n_k$$

**유일성**: $n_i$들은 유일하게 결정됨

### Primary Decomposition Form^[일차 분해 형태]

$$G \cong \prod_{p \text{ prime}} G_p$$

where $G_p$ is the $p$-primary component

$$G_p \cong \mathbb{Z}/p^{a_1}\mathbb{Z} \times \cdots \times \mathbb{Z}/p^{a_m}\mathbb{Z}$$

**Chinese Remainder Theorem**과 동등

자세한 내용은 [[Isomorphism]] 참조

## Cauchy's Theorem (Abelian case)

$G$ finite abelian, $p \mid |G|$ (prime)

$$\Rightarrow \exists g \in G \text{ with } |g| = p$$

**일반**: Non-abelian의 경우도 성립 (Cauchy's theorem)

## Lagrange's Theorem

$H \leq G$ finite abelian

$$|H| \mid |G|$$

**역 (Abelian case)**: $n \mid |G| \Rightarrow \exists H \leq G$ with $|H| = n$

**주의**: 일반 group에서는 역이 성립하지 않음!

## Abelian Simple Groups

**정리**: Abelian group이 simple $\Leftrightarrow$ Cyclic of prime order

$$G \text{ abelian and simple} \Leftrightarrow G \cong \mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z}$$

**증명**: 
- Abelian이므로 모든 subgroup normal
- Simple이므로 proper nontrivial subgroup 없음
- Lagrange $\Rightarrow$ prime order
- Prime order $\Rightarrow$ cyclic ✓

자세한 내용은 [[Simple Group]] 참조

## Pontryagin Duality

Locally compact abelian group $G$에 대해:

$$\hat{G} = \text{Hom}(G, S^1)$$ 

**Dual group**^[쌍대군]

**정리**: $({\hat{G}})^{\wedge} \cong G$ (Double dual)

**예**:
- $\hat{\mathbb{Z}} \cong S^1$
- $\hat{S^1} \cong \mathbb{Z}$
- $\hat{\mathbb{R}} \cong \mathbb{R}$

**응용**: Fourier analysis

자세한 내용은 [[Double dual]] 참조

# <span class="header-remark">Remark</span>

## 직관적 이해

**Abelian = "순서가 중요하지 않음"**

$$ab = ba \Rightarrow \text{연산 순서 자유}$$

### 메타포: 덧셈

숫자 더하기: $2 + 3 = 3 + 2$

**자연스러운 연산**: Most "natural" operations are abelian

### 메타포: 벡터 더하기

$$\vec{v} + \vec{w} = \vec{w} + \vec{v}$$

**Parallelogram law**: 순서 무관

## 왜 중요한가?

### 1. 가장 단순한 Groups

**구조가 잘 알려짐**:
- Finitely generated: 완전 분류
- Infinite: Pontryagin duality

### 2. Building Blocks

**모든 group의 "abelian part"**:
- Abelianization $G/G'$
- Center $Z(G)$

### 3. Linear Algebra

**Vector spaces = Abelian groups + scalar multiplication**

선형대수학의 모든 결과 활용 가능

### 4. 응용 분야

**어디서나 등장**:
- Number theory (class groups)
- Topology (homology groups)
- Algebraic geometry (Picard groups)
- Physics (gauge symmetries)

## Abelian vs Non-Abelian

| | **Abelian** | **Non-Abelian** |
|---|---|---|
| **Commutativity** | $ab = ba$ | $ab \neq ba$ (일반적) |
| **Normal subgroups** | All subgroups | Not all |
| **Quotients** | Always exist | Need normal subgroups |
| **Classification** | Well-understood | Very difficult |
| **Simple groups** | $\mathbb{Z}/p\mathbb{Z}$ only | Many families |
| **예** | $\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$ | $S_n, D_n, A_n$ |

## 표기법 선택

### Additive Notation (Abelian)

- 연산: $a + b$
- Identity: $0$
- Inverse: $-a$
- $n$-fold: $na = a + a + \cdots + a$

**사용**: Vector spaces, $\mathbb{Z}$, modules

### Multiplicative Notation (General)

- 연산: $ab$ or $a \cdot b$
- Identity: $e$ or $1$
- Inverse: $a^{-1}$
- $n$-fold: $a^n = a \cdot a \cdots a$

**사용**: General groups, $\mathbb{R}^\times$

## Abelian의 어원

**Niels Henrik Abel** (1802-1829):
- 노르웨이 수학자
- 5차 방정식의 해의 공식 불가능 증명
- Abel-Ruffini theorem
- 26세 사망 (가난과 질병)

**Abelian**: Abel을 기리기 위한 이름

**역사적**: Commutative라는 용어도 사용되지만, Abelian이 더 일반적

## 관련 성질들

**Abelian group은 자동으로**:
- Nilpotent (nilpotency class 1)
- Solvable (derived length 1)
- 모든 subgroup이 characteristic

**하지만 자동은 아님**:
- Finitely generated
- Torsion-free
- Divisible
