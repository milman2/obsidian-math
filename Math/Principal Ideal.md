# <span class="header-definition">Definition</span>

## Principal Ideal^[주 아이디얼]

### In Commutative Ring

Commutative ring $R$에서 원소 $a \in R$로 **generated^[생성]**된 ideal:

$$\langle a \rangle = aR = Ra = \{ra : r \in R\}$$

**표기**: $\langle a \rangle$, $aR$, $(a)$

**의미**: $a$의 모든 배수들의 집합

**정의**: **Principal ideal^[주 아이디얼]** = 하나의 원소로 생성된 ideal

### In Non-commutative Ring

**Left principal ideal**:

$$Ra = \{ra : r \in R\}$$

**Right principal ideal**:

$$aR = \{ar : r \in R\}$$

**Two-sided principal ideal**:

$$\langle a \rangle = RaR = \left\{ \sum_{i} r_i a s_i : r_i, s_i \in R \right\}$$

**주의**: Commutative에서만 일치!

본 문서는 주로 **commutative rings** 다룸

## Principal Ideal Generated by Multiple Elements

**NOT principal**: $\langle a, b \rangle$ (generally)

$$\langle a, b \rangle = \{ra + sb : r, s \in R\}$$

**예외**: $\langle a, b \rangle = \langle d \rangle$ if $d = \gcd(a, b)$ (in PID)

### Example

$\mathbb{Z}$에서:
- $\langle 6, 9 \rangle = \langle 3 \rangle$ ✓ (principal!)
- $\langle 4, 6 \rangle = \langle 2 \rangle$ ✓

하지만 $\mathbb{Z}[x]$에서:
- $\langle 2, x \rangle$ is NOT principal ✗

## Principal Ideal Domain (PID)^[주 아이디얼 정역]

**정의**: Integral domain^[정역]where **every ideal is principal**

$$\forall I \triangleleft R: \quad \exists a \in R, \quad I = \langle a \rangle$$

**예**: $\mathbb{Z}$, $k[x]$ (field $k$), $\mathbb{Z}[i]$

자세한 내용은 [[Principal Ideal Domain]] 참조

## Principal Ideal Ring (PIR)

**정의**: Ring (not necessarily domain) where every ideal is principal

**예**: $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$

**차이**: PIR은 zero divisors 가능, PID는 불가

# <span class="header-properties">Properties</span>

## Basic Properties

### 1. Smallest Ideal Containing $a$

**정리**: $\langle a \rangle$는 $a$를 포함하는 가장 작은 ideal

**증명**: 
- $a = 1 \cdot a \in \langle a \rangle$ ✓
- $I \supseteq \{a\}$이고 $I$ ideal이면:
  - 모든 $r \in R$에 대해 $ra \in I$ (ideal property)
  - 따라서 $\langle a \rangle \subseteq I$ ✓

### 2. Generator는 유일하지 않음

**정리**: $\langle a \rangle = \langle b \rangle$ $\Leftrightarrow$ $a = ub$ where $u$ is unit

**증명**:

($\Rightarrow$) $\langle a \rangle = \langle b \rangle$이면
- $a \in \langle b \rangle \Rightarrow a = rb$
- $b \in \langle a \rangle \Rightarrow b = sa$
- $\Rightarrow$ $a = r(sa) = (rs)a$
- $\Rightarrow$ $rs = 1$ (if $a \neq 0$)
- 따라서 $r, s$ are units ✓

($\Leftarrow$) $a = ub$ (unit $u$)이면
- $\langle a \rangle \subseteq \langle b \rangle$: $a = ub \in \langle b \rangle$
- $\langle b \rangle \subseteq \langle a \rangle$: $b = u^{-1}a \in \langle a \rangle$
- 따라서 $\langle a \rangle = \langle b \rangle$ ✓

**예**: $\mathbb{Z}$에서 $\langle 5 \rangle = \langle -5 \rangle$

### 3. Operations

**Sum**:

$$\langle a \rangle + \langle b \rangle = \langle a, b \rangle$$

(NOT generally principal!)

**Product**:

$$\langle a \rangle \cdot \langle b \rangle = \langle ab \rangle$$

**증명**: 
- $\langle a \rangle \cdot \langle b \rangle = \{\sum r_i a \cdot s_j b\} = \{\sum t_{ij} ab\} = \langle ab \rangle$ ✓

**Intersection** (in PID):

$$\langle a \rangle \cap \langle b \rangle = \langle \text{lcm}(a, b) \rangle$$

### 4. Inclusion

**정리**: $\langle a \rangle \subseteq \langle b \rangle$ $\Leftrightarrow$ $b | a$

**증명**:

($\Rightarrow$) $a \in \langle b \rangle \Rightarrow a = rb \Rightarrow b | a$ ✓

($\Leftarrow$) $b | a \Rightarrow a = rb \Rightarrow \langle a \rangle \subseteq \langle b \rangle$ ✓

**예**: $\langle 6 \rangle \subseteq \langle 2 \rangle$ in $\mathbb{Z}$ (since $2 | 6$)

### 5. Principal Ideal in Quotient

**정리**: $\pi: R \to R/I$ canonical projection이면

$$\pi(\langle a \rangle) = \langle \pi(a) \rangle$$

**의미**: Principal ideals는 quotient에서도 principal

## Prime and Maximal Principal Ideals

### Prime Principal Ideal

**정리**: $\langle a \rangle$ is prime $\Leftrightarrow$ $a$ is prime element

**정의**: $a$가 **prime element^[소원]**이다 $\Leftrightarrow$
1. $a$ is non-unit, non-zero
2. $a | bc \Rightarrow a | b$ or $a | c$

**예**: $\mathbb{Z}$에서 $\langle p \rangle$ (prime $p$)

자세한 내용은 [[Prime Ideal]], [[Prime Element]] 참조

### Maximal Principal Ideal

**In PID**: Nonzero prime ideal = maximal

**정리**: $R$ PID이고 $\langle a \rangle \neq \{0\}$ prime이면 $\langle a \rangle$ maximal

**증명**: $\langle a \rangle \subseteq \langle b \rangle$이면
- $b | a \Rightarrow a = bc$
- $a$ prime $\Rightarrow$ $a | b$ or $a | c$
- If $a | b$: $\langle b \rangle = R$
- If $a | c$: $\langle b \rangle = \langle a \rangle$
- 따라서 maximal ✓

자세한 내용은 [[Maximal Ideal]] 참조

## GCD and LCM in PIDs

**정리**: PID $R$에서 $\langle a \rangle + \langle b \rangle = \langle d \rangle$이면 $d = \gcd(a, b)$

**정리**: PID $R$에서 $\langle a \rangle \cap \langle b \rangle = \langle m \rangle$이면 $m = \text{lcm}(a, b)$

**의미**: GCD와 LCM을 ideal operations로 표현!

# <span class="header-examples">Examples</span>

## Example 1: $\mathbb{Z}$

**모든 ideal은 principal**! (PID)

$$I = n\mathbb{Z} = \langle n \rangle$$

**예**:
- $\langle 2 \rangle = \{\ldots, -4, -2, 0, 2, 4, \ldots\}$ (even integers)
- $\langle 5 \rangle = \{5k : k \in \mathbb{Z}\}$
- $\langle 0 \rangle = \{0\}$ (zero ideal)
- $\langle 1 \rangle = \mathbb{Z}$ (whole ring)

**Operations**:
- $\langle 2 \rangle + \langle 3 \rangle = \langle 1 \rangle = \mathbb{Z}$ ✓
- $\langle 2 \rangle \cdot \langle 3 \rangle = \langle 6 \rangle$ ✓
- $\langle 4 \rangle \cap \langle 6 \rangle = \langle 12 \rangle$ (lcm!) ✓

## Example 2: $k[x]$ (Polynomial Ring)

**Field $k$에서**: 모든 ideal은 principal! (PID)

$$I = \langle f(x) \rangle$$

**예**:
- $\langle x \rangle = \{x \cdot p(x) : p(x) \in k[x]\}$
- $\langle x^2 + 1 \rangle$ in $\mathbb{R}[x]$
- $\langle x - a \rangle$ (linear polynomials)

**Prime ideals**: $\langle f \rangle$ where $f$ irreducible

**Maximal ideals**: All nonzero prime ideals (PID!)

## Example 3: $\mathbb{Z}[i]$ (Gaussian Integers)

**모든 ideal은 principal**! (PID)

**예**:
- $\langle 2 \rangle$ (not prime! $2 = (1+i)(1-i)$)
- $\langle 1 + i \rangle$ (prime!)
- $\langle 3 \rangle$ (prime!)
- $\langle 2 + i \rangle$ (prime!)

**Norm**: $N(a + bi) = a^2 + b^2$

자세한 내용은 [[Gaussian Integers]] 참조

## Example 4: $\mathbb{Z}[x]$ (NON-example)

**NOT all ideals are principal!** (NOT PID)

**Principal ideals**:
- $\langle 2 \rangle = \{2p(x) : p(x) \in \mathbb{Z}[x]\}$ ✓
- $\langle x \rangle = \{xp(x) : p(x) \in \mathbb{Z}[x]\}$ ✓
- $\langle x^2 + 1 \rangle$ ✓

**NOT principal**:
- $\langle 2, x \rangle = \{2p(x) + xq(x) : p, q \in \mathbb{Z}[x]\}$ ✗

**증명 (not principal)**:
- Suppose $\langle 2, x \rangle = \langle f(x) \rangle$
- Then $2, x \in \langle f \rangle$
- $\Rightarrow$ $f | 2$ and $f | x$ in $\mathbb{Z}[x]$
- $\Rightarrow$ $f \in \mathbb{Z}$ (constant)
- $\Rightarrow$ $f = \pm 1$ or $f = \pm 2$
- If $f = \pm 1$: $\langle f \rangle = \mathbb{Z}[x]$ ✗
- If $f = \pm 2$: $x \notin \langle 2 \rangle$ ✗
- Contradiction! ✓

## Example 5: $k[x, y]$ (Two Variables)

**NOT PID** (multiple variables)

**Principal ideals**:
- $\langle x \rangle$ ✓
- $\langle y \rangle$ ✓
- $\langle x^2 + y^2 \rangle$ ✓
- $\langle xy \rangle$ ✓

**NOT principal**:
- $\langle x, y \rangle$ ✗ (no common divisor except units)

**하지만 UFD**: Unique factorization ✓

## Example 6: $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$

**모든 ideal은 principal**! (PIR, not PID if $n$ composite)

**예 ($n = 12$)**:
- $\langle 1 \rangle = \mathbb{Z}/12\mathbb{Z}$
- $\langle 2 \rangle = \{0, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10\}$
- $\langle 3 \rangle = \{0, 3, 6, 9\}$
- $\langle 4 \rangle = \{0, 4, 8\}$
- $\langle 6 \rangle = \{0, 6\}$

**Lattice structure**:

$$\langle 1 \rangle \supseteq \langle 2 \rangle, \langle 3 \rangle \supseteq \langle 6 \rangle$$

## Example 7: $\mathbb{Q}[x, y]$ with Ideal

**Consider**: $I = \langle x^2, xy, y^2 \rangle$

**Question**: Is $I$ principal?

**Answer**: NO!

**Reason**: 
- If $I = \langle f \rangle$, then $f | x^2$, $f | xy$, $f | y^2$
- In $\mathbb{Q}[x, y]$ (UFD), this forces $f \in \mathbb{Q}$ or $f | x$ and $f | y$
- But $x \notin I$ and $y \notin I$
- Contradiction! ✓

## Example 8: Matrix Ring (Non-commutative)

**$M_2(\mathbb{R})$**: Matrix ring

**Left principal ideal**:

$$Re = \left\{ \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix} : a, b \in \mathbb{R} \right\}$$

where $e = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$

**NOT two-sided**! (not commutative)

## Example 9: Localization

**$R = \mathbb{Z}$, $S = \mathbb{Z} \setminus 2\mathbb{Z}$**

**Localization**: $S^{-1}\mathbb{Z} = \mathbb{Z}_{(2)}$

**Principal ideals**:
- $\left\langle \frac{2}{1} \right\rangle$ (unique maximal ideal)
- $\left\langle \frac{3}{1} \right\rangle = S^{-1}\mathbb{Z}$ (unit!)

**모든 ideal principal** (localization of PID is PID)

## Example 10: Power Series

**$k[[x]]$**: Formal power series over field $k$

**Principal ideals**:
- $\langle x \rangle$ (maximal ideal)
- $\langle x^2 \rangle$
- $\langle x^n \rangle$ for $n \geq 0$

**모든 ideal은 principal**: $I = \langle x^n \rangle$ for some $n$

**PID**: ✓ (actually, DVR = discrete valuation ring)

자세한 내용은 [[Discrete Valuation Ring]] 참조

# <span class="header-theorem">Theorem</span>

## Structure Theorem for PIDs

**정리**: $R$ PID이고 $M$ finitely generated $R$-module이면

$$M \cong R^r \oplus R/(d_1) \oplus \cdots \oplus R/(d_n)$$

where $d_1 | d_2 | \cdots | d_n$

**응용**: Classification of finitely generated abelian groups

자세한 내용은 [[Principal Ideal Domain]] 참조

## PID $\Rightarrow$ UFD

**정리**: Every PID is a UFD

**증명 개요**:
1. Irreducibles exist (ACC on principal ideals)
2. Factorization exists (induction)
3. Uniqueness: irreducible $\Rightarrow$ prime (in PID)

자세한 내용은 [[Unique Factorization Domain]] 참조

## Euclidean Domain $\Rightarrow$ PID

**정리**: Every Euclidean domain is a PID

**증명**: Division algorithm으로 generator 찾기 ✓

자세한 내용은 [[Euclidean Domain]] 참조

## Dedekind Domain Characterization

**정리**: $R$ Dedekind domain $\Leftrightarrow$
- Noetherian
- Integrally closed
- Dimension 1
- Every nonzero prime is maximal

**특징**: 모든 ideal이 uniquely factor into primes (ideal factorization)

**예**: $\mathcal{O}_K$ (ring of integers in number field)

자세한 내용은 [[Dedekind Domain]] 참조

## Chinese Remainder Theorem

**정리**: $\langle a_1 \rangle, \ldots, \langle a_n \rangle$ pairwise coprime이면

$$R / (\langle a_1 \rangle \cap \cdots \cap \langle a_n \rangle) \cong R/\langle a_1 \rangle \times \cdots \times R/\langle a_n \rangle$$

**Coprime**: $\langle a_i \rangle + \langle a_j \rangle = R$ for $i \neq j$

**응용**: $\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}$ decomposition

자세한 내용은 [[Chinese Remainder Theorem]] 참조

# <span class="header-remark">Remark</span>

## 직관적 이해

**Principal Ideal = "하나로 생성"**

### 메타포: 배수

**정수 $n$의 배수**: $\{\ldots, -2n, -n, 0, n, 2n, \ldots\}$

**Principal ideal $\langle a \rangle$**: $a$의 "배수들"

**의미**:
- 하나의 원소 $a$로 시작
- 모든 "$a$의 배수" 포함
- "단순한" 구조

### Generators

**같은 ideal, 다른 generator**:

$$\langle a \rangle = \langle b \rangle \Leftrightarrow a = ub \text{ (unit } u \text{)}$$

**예**: $\langle 5 \rangle = \langle -5 \rangle$ in $\mathbb{Z}$

**의미**: Generator는 "unit multiple"까지 유일

## Principal vs General Ideal

| | Principal Ideal | General Ideal |
|---|-----------------|---------------|
| **Generators** | One | Possibly many |
| **형태** | $\langle a \rangle$ | $\langle a_1, \ldots, a_n \rangle$ |
| **예 ($\mathbb{Z}$)** | $\langle 6 \rangle$ | All are principal! |
| **예 ($\mathbb{Z}[x]$)** | $\langle 2 \rangle$, $\langle x \rangle$ | $\langle 2, x \rangle$ (not principal) |
| **구조** | Simple | Can be complex |

**핵심**: 
- Principal ideals는 "가장 단순한" ideals
- Not all ideals are principal (except in PIDs)

## PID의 중요성

**Principal Ideal Domain**:

$$\text{Every ideal is principal}$$

**장점**:
1. **Simple structure**: 모든 ideal은 하나의 원소로 기술
2. **GCD/LCM**: Ideal operations로 계산 가능
3. **Factorization**: Unique factorization (UFD)
4. **Module theory**: Classification theorem

**예**:
- $\mathbb{Z}$: Number theory의 기초
- $k[x]$: Polynomial algebra
- $\mathbb{Z}[i]$: Gaussian integers

자세한 내용은 [[Principal Ideal Domain]] 참조

## 응용 분야

### 1. Number Theory

**$\mathbb{Z}$**: Principal ideal domain

**Ideals**: $n\mathbb{Z}$

**응용**:
- GCD and LCM
- Diophantine equations
- Modular arithmetic

### 2. Polynomial Algebra

**$k[x]$**: Polynomials over field

**Applications**:
- Polynomial division
- Irreducible polynomials
- Algebraic extensions

### 3. Algebraic Number Theory

**$\mathcal{O}_K$**: Ring of integers

**Ideal factorization**: Even when element factorization fails

**Example**: $\mathbb{Z}[\sqrt{-5}]$ (not PID, but Dedekind)

### 4. Module Theory

**PID modules**: Finitely generated modules classified

**Structure theorem**: Direct sum decomposition

**Applications**: Jordan normal form, abelian groups

### 5. Computer Algebra

**Gröbner bases**: Generalization to non-PIDs

**Ideal membership**: Computational methods

## 계산 방법

### Generate Principal Ideal

**Input**: Element $a \in R$

**Output**: $\langle a \rangle = \{ra : r \in R\}$

**In practice**: 
- $\mathbb{Z}$: $n\mathbb{Z}$ (multiples)
- $k[x]$: $\{f(x) \cdot g(x) : g \in k[x]\}$

### Check if Principal

**Input**: Ideal $I$

**Question**: $I = \langle a \rangle$ for some $a$?

**Method**:
1. If $I = \{0\}$: $I = \langle 0 \rangle$ ✓
2. Find "smallest" nonzero element (Euclidean algorithm in ED)
3. Check if it generates $I$

**In PID**: Always principal!

**In general**: May need Gröbner basis methods

### Operations

**Sum**: $\langle a \rangle + \langle b \rangle$
- In PID: $= \langle \gcd(a, b) \rangle$
- In general: $= \langle a, b \rangle$ (may not be principal)

**Product**: $\langle a \rangle \cdot \langle b \rangle = \langle ab \rangle$ ✓ (always)

**Intersection**: $\langle a \rangle \cap \langle b \rangle$
- In PID: $= \langle \text{lcm}(a, b) \rangle$

## Common Pitfall^[흔한 실수]

### 1. Not All Ideals Principal

$\langle 2, x \rangle$ in $\mathbb{Z}[x]$ is NOT principal!

**실수**: "Every ideal은 principal"이라고 가정

**사실**: PID에서만!

### 2. Generator 유일성

$\langle 5 \rangle = \langle -5 \rangle$ in $\mathbb{Z}$

**실수**: Generator가 유일하다고 생각

**사실**: Unit multiple까지만 유일

### 3. Sum of Principals

$\langle 2 \rangle + \langle 3 \rangle = \langle 1 \rangle = \mathbb{Z}$ ✓

**실수**: $\langle 2 + 3 \rangle = \langle 5 \rangle$이라고 생각 ✗

**사실**: Sum $\neq$ $\langle a + b \rangle$

### 4. PIR vs PID

**PIR**: Principal Ideal Ring (모든 ideal principal)

**PID**: Principal Ideal Domain (integral domain + PIR)

**차이**: PID는 zero divisors 없음

예: $\mathbb{Z}/4\mathbb{Z}$ is PIR but not PID

### 5. Quotient

$\langle a \rangle / \langle b \rangle$?

**의미 불명확**: Ideal quotient $\langle a \rangle : \langle b \rangle$?

아니면 quotient ring $R/\langle b \rangle$에서 image?

**명확히 할 것!**
